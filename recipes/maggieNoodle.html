MAGGI Chicken Biryani Noodles Recipe

Biryani or your favourite MAGGI Masala Noodles? Difficult choice to make? Well, we just made it easier for you with our epic MAGGI Noodles Biryani Recipe. By the way, when you master this one, it will add a seriously heavy feather to your hat of culinary skills! Challenge accepted?

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)
  • 2 Teaspoon Oil
  • 4 Green cardamom
  • 2 Cinnamon (1 Inch Pieces)
  • 8 Cloves
  • 3 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Teaspoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 3 Tomato (Finely chopped)
  • 1 Teaspoon Red chilli powder
  • 2 Teaspoon Coriander Powder
  • 1 Teaspoonk Saunf Powder
  • 500gm Chicken Sausage
  • 1 Cup Peas (Shelled)
  • 1 Pinch Salt (To Taste)
  • 0.5 Cup Coriander Leaves (Chopped)
  • 0.5 Cup Mint Leaves (Chopped)
  • 0.5 Cup Fried Onion

Instruction

Step 1

Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.

Step 2

Heat oil in a pan. Add the green cardamom, cinnamon and cloves. Then, add the onions and ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for 5 minutes. Add the tomatoes and cook till they are soft.

Step 3

Add the red chilli powder, coriander powder and saunf powder, fry for a minute. Add the sliced sausages, and saute for 5 minutes. Add salt to taste.

Step 4

Cook MAGGI Masala Noodles as per instructions on the pack. In a serving dish put half the cooked MAGGI Noodles, then put half the cooked sausages. Now, put the remaining MAGGI Noodles and cooked sausages

Step 5

Serve hot garnished with coriander leaves, mint leaves and fried onions.
